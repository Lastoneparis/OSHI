A: No! OSHI generates a cryptographic identity automatically. No registration required. Q: Can OSHI read my messages? A: No! All messages are end-to-end encrypted. Only you and your recipient can read them. Q: Do you collect my data? A: No! We collect zero personal data. No phone number, no email, no tracking. Q: Is OSHI really open source? A: Yes! Our code is available on GitHub for anyone to audit. Q: Can I use OSHI without Internet? A: Yes! The local mesh mode works directly between iPhones without any network. Technical Questions Q: What encryption does OSHI use? A: Double Ratchet protocol (Signal protocol) with AES-256-GCM and Curve25519. Q: Is OSHI compatible with Signal/WhatsApp? A: No, OSHI is a separate network. You need OSHI on both devices. Q: How long are messages stored? A: Messages are stored locally on your device. Cloud messages on IPFS are available for 30 days, then automatically deleted. Q: Can I export my messages? A: Currently messages are stored only on your device. Backup features coming soon. Q: What is IPFS? A: InterPlanetary File System - a decentralized storage network.
